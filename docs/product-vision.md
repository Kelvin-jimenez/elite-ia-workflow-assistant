# Product Vision

## Product Name

Elite IA Workflow Assistant

## Vision

Elite IA Workflow Assistant is a project designed to automate client request management using AI, workflows and business process automation.

The goal is to help small businesses, freelancers and digital service providers reduce repetitive manual work when receiving, classifying and responding to client requests.

## Target Users

- Freelancers
- Small businesses
- Digital service providers
- Support service providers
- Automation consultants
- AI service providers

## Main Problem

Many small businesses receive client requests from different channels such as email, WhatsApp, marketplaces, forms or social media.

This creates:

- Disorganized information
- Slow response times
- Repetitive manual work
- Lost opportunities
- Poor follow-up

## Product Value

Elite IA Workflow Assistant will help by:

- Receiving client requests in a structured way
- Storing information automatically
- Classifying requests with AI
- Creating summaries
- Suggesting responses
- Supporting human decision-making before sending anything

## Product Approach

The product will start as a simple MVP using no-code and low-code tools such as n8n, Google Forms, Google Sheets and AI APIs.

Future versions may include RAG, dashboards, CRM features and more advanced AI agents.
